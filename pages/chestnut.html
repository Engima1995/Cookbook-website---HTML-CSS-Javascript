Chestnut & Mustard Parcels

Can be served on their own as a hot or cold canapé, or with Betroot & Leek Consomme.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ leek, washed and finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 medium tomato, skinned and finely chopped
  • 25g vacuum packed cooked chestnuts
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3 sheets filo pastry, defrosted
  • Extra olive oil for brushing filo pastry

Method

Steps
1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
2. In a small pan fry the leek and garlic in oil. Add tomato, chestnuts, mustard and paprika and cook on gentle heat for 10 minutes, stirring frequently.
3. Cut filo pastry into 8 rectangles 10” x 5” each. Brush each one with olive oil and fold in half. Place 1 ½ tsp of filling in centre of each piece filo and bring sides up, twisting the top.
4. Place on lightly oiled baking sheet and bake in oven for 15 minutes.
5. Serve hot – placing one parcel in centre of Betroot & Leek Consommé if making the recipes together. 6. Keep remainder for serving with drinks.
